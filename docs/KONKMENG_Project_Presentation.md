## SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM (1 minute)

### Why Khmer Developers Need KONKMENG

**4 Major Challenges:**

1. **Language Barrier**
   - 90% of coding resources are in English
   - Khmer developers spend hours translating
   - Difficult to understand error messages

2. **Slow Debugging**
   - Beginners waste 2-3 hours on simple errors
   - No instant help available
   - Trial and error takes too long

3. **No Khmer Teachers**
   - Only 1 Khmer code teacher per 1,000 students
   - Expensive private tutors ($20-50/hour)
   - Limited access to quality education

4. **English-Only AI Tools**
   - ChatGPT, Copilot only explain in English
   - Not helpful for Khmer beginners
   - Creates frustration and confusion

**Impact: 50,000+ Khmer developers struggle daily**

## SLIDE 7: AI PROMPT ENGINEERING (1 minute)

### The Secret Sauce

**Custom AI Prompts Power KONKMENG**

**2 Main System Prompts:**

1. **Khmer Prompt**
   - 100% Khmer language
   - Khmer technical terms
   - Cambodian cultural context
   - Real-life analogies (បណ្ណាល័យ, សាងជញ្ជាំង, ធ្វើម្ហូប)

2. **English Prompt**
   - Professional terminology
   - International standards
   - Real-life analogies (library, cooking, building)

**5 Prompt Engineering Techniques:**
- Role-based prompting (AI acts as teacher)
- Structured output formatting (consistent sections)
- Template binding (parseable patterns)
- Constraint setting (no repetition, quality rules)
- Mandatory analogies (easy to understand)

**Result: Professional, consistent, educational responses**
